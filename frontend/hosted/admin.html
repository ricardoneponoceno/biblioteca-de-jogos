Site owner

Owners keep a name, character colour, and badge colour that stick — they survive resets and reconnects. Crown a visitor under Moderation to add an owner, then customise their look here.

Connected towns

Link neighbouring TownSquares to the left or right edge of your square. A signpost appears on that side, and visitors can walk over to travel there. Copy the refreshed snippet below after you save.

Moderation

Bot protection requires each visitor's browser to solve a small proof-of-work before joining. It stops automated scripts while staying invisible to real visitors. Enable it only if you are being hit by bots, as it adds a brief delay when visitors arrive.
